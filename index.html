Dice Drop Match

Match dice & beat the clock

Free Puzzle iPhone 4+

Dice Drop Match is a fast-paced puzzle arcade game: drag and match dice blocks to make them fall, clear space on the board, and stay ahead of the timer. Match dice with the same numbers to remove tiles, keep the stack under control, and chase a higher score. As you play, the pace tightens—quick thinking, sharp reflexes, and smart placement keep the board from filling up.

Every second counts. Grab bonus time when it appears to extend your run and push your best score. Simple controls, smooth drops, and bite-sized sessions make it easy to play one more round.

How it plays

  • Match identical dice values to clear groups and open the board.
  • Let matched blocks fall away before the stack reaches the limit.
  • Race the clock; bonus pickups can add precious seconds.
  • Climb toward your personal high score with each session.

Why it clicks

  • Time pressure that rewards speed without harsh complexity.
  • Match-and-clear mechanics that are easy to learn, satisfying to master.
  • Arcade flow built for short breaks or long streaks.
  • Designed for iPhone—pick up and play anywhere.
